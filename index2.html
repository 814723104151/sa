About Us

Stea is more than just a bubble tea shop – it is a space where flavor meets happiness. Inspired by modern café culture and pastel aesthetics, we bring you handcrafted drinks made with premium tea leaves, fresh fruits, creamy blends, and chewy boba pearls.

Every cup at Stea is prepared with care, creativity, and consistency. Our goal is to create a cozy and vibrant atmosphere where friends gather, memories are made, and every sip feels refreshing and satisfying.

Whether you love classic milk tea or fruity refreshers, Stea promises sweet, sassy, and unforgettable bubble tea vibes.
